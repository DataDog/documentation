## Overview

With Verisign OpenHybrid™ Integration, Datadog monitors signal threat information back to the Verisign DDoS protection cloud to provide you with faster, more efficient detection and mitigation of DDoS attacks

## Setup

To take advantage of the Verisign OpenHybrid™ service, you must be a customer of Verisign DDoS Protection Service. For more information about Verisign DDoS Protection Services, contact a Verisign representative by phone at `866-367-0095` or `1-703-948-4140`, by email at websales@verisign.com or visit us at `VerisignInc.com/ddos`.

### Configuration

Verisign DDoS Protection Service customers will receive a copy of the Verisign Integration user guide which provides details on configuring the Verisign OpenHybrid™ service.  

Please refer to that guide to complete [the Verisign OpenHybrid™ Integration configuration in Datadog](https://app.datadoghq.com/account/settings#integrations/verisign_openhybrid) by setting up your `Account ID`,  your `API key` and your `application name`
