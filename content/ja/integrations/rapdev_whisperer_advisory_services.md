## Overview

Our Datadog engineering expertise... customized and on demand!

RapDev, a Datadog Gold Partner, has extensive experience leveraging the full power of Datadog's application and infrastructure monitoring capabilities. Our Whisperer offering gives you access to the full depth of that expertise with more flexibility than ever.

Whisperer offers two tiers of service:

- **Essential**: Unlimited Slack support, unlimited support ticket entry, Zoom calls (billed per hour), discount on RapDev's Usage Tracker, and additional discounts on other RapDev Datadog Marketplace integrations
- **Pro**: Unlimited Slack support, unlimited support ticket entry, unlimited Zoom calls included, additional discount on RapDev's Usage Tracker, and additional discounts on other RapDev Datadog Marketplace integrations

The benefits of this service include:

- **Accelerated time to value**: De-risk and accelerate your internal team’s efforts with Datadog
- **Designed for your roadmap**: On-demand support to meet the requirements of your roadmap
- **Market-leading experience**: Access our team's expertise outside the structure of a project-based engagement
