## Overview

After Workload Protection surfaces runtime risk through Agent events, signals, and findings, **Respond and Report** is where you configure enforcement, drive remediation, and measure outcomes. This documentation describes the main response surfaces in Workload Protection and how they connect to the rest of the product.

## Active Protection

[Active Protection][1] turns supported high-confidence detections into **enforcement** on the host (for example blocking or terminating crypto-mining activity) instead of monitoring only.

## Remediation

[Remediation][4] covers how you **act on detections** after a signal was generated. You can directly take actions from the signal side pannel to protect your infrastructure.
