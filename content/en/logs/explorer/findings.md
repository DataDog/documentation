## Overview

Findings helps you capture, organize, and reuse important context during log investigations in [Log Explorer][1].

With Findings, you can save log lines, visualizations, and queries to a canvas while investigating. This allows you to return to them or branch into new questions without losing track of earlier observations or investigative paths.

Use Findings to collect possible evidence, compare results, and build context as your investigation evolves.
