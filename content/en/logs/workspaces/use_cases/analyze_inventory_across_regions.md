## Overview

This guide provides a step-by-step process for using Log Workspaces to analyze retail inventory, focusing on identifying products with low stock and high sales across different regions. By joining inventory, sales, and location data, retailers can make informed decisions about restocking and ensure high-demand products are available to customers.
