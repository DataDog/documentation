## Overview

Segmentation is a feature in Product Analytics that allows you to analyze and understand specific groups or segments of your user base. By segmenting your users based on various characteristics and behaviors, you can uncover valuable insights, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimize your product and user experience.
