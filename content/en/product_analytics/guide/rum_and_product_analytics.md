## What is Product Analytics?

Product Analytics is a dedicated product offering that launched in limited availability on June 25, 2024. It is a new product built for product managers, product owners, engineering leaders, and others who want to leverage real user behavior to derive product and business insights, without requiring knowledge of other Datadog observability products.
