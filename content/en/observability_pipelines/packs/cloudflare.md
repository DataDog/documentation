## Overview

{{< img src="observability_pipelines/packs/cloudflare.png" alt="The Cloudflare pack" style="width:25%;" >}}

Cloudflare logs show edge traffic, performance, and security.

What this pack does:

- Deduplicates traffic, samples low-risk logs
- Keeps key requests and security events
- Filters secrets and removes sensitive fields
