## Overview

{{< img src="observability_pipelines/packs/akamai_cdn.png" alt="The Akamai pack" style="width:25%;" >}}

Akamai logs show client requests and responses at the edge.

What this pack does:

- Drops static files, health checks, and bot noise
- Keeps failed, risky, and unusual requests
- Normalizes key values for analysis
