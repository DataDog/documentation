## Overview

{{< img src="observability_pipelines/packs/f5.png" alt="The F5 pack" style="width:25%;" >}}

F5 logs capture traffic, security policy, and intrusion events.

What this pack does:

- Keeps blocked, denied, and intrusion alerts
- Samples high-volume traffic categories
- Drops routine allow events and health checks
