## Overview

{{< img src="observability_pipelines/packs/juniper_srx_traffic.png" alt="The Juniper SRX Firewall Traffic Logs pack" style="width:25%;" >}}

Juniper SRX Firewall records network session data.

What this pack does:

- Parses RT_FLOW session events into normalized fields
- Extracts IPs, ports, and traffic counters for analytics
- Drops low value routine session logs
