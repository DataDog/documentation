## Delete a GCP integration

Delete a given Datadog-GCP integration.

**ARGUMENTS**:

* **`project_id`** [*required*]:

    Your Google Cloud project ID found in your JSON service account key.

* **`client_email`** [*required*]:

    Your email found in your JSON service account key.
