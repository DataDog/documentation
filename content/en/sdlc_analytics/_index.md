## Overview

SDLC Analytics provides tools to measure your software delivery performance and the impact of AI coding assistants on your development workflow.

- [DORA Metrics][1]: Track deployment frequency, change lead time, change failure rate, and failed deployment recovery time.
- [AI Impact][2]: Measure how AI coding assistants affect your software delivery performance.
