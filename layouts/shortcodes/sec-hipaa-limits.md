<div class="alert alert-info">Datadog does not send security notifications through webhooks due to HIPAA restrictions. In your Datadog notifications, if you reference any security weakness in <b>Finding</b> or any Security product in <b>Signals</b>, you cannot use <code>@webhook...</code> in the <b>Notify the following recipients</b> setting. Security alerts won't be sent to the webhook for HIPAA-enabled accounts. If you want these alerts sent, please reach out to your Datadog account team to seek an exception.</div>
